## What we collect

If you subscribe to our newsletter, we collect the email address you provide. We don't collect names, payment information, or other personal data through this site.

This site does not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics by default. If that changes, this policy will be updated to reflect it.

## How we use it

Your email address is used only to send you updates about Cubic - devlogs, release announcements, and similar news. We don't sell or share your email address with third parties.

## Your choices

Every newsletter email includes an unsubscribe link. You can also email us at <contact.twinturtlegames@gmail.com> to request that your data be deleted.

## Embedded content

Trailer video is embedded from YouTube using its privacy-enhanced mode, which limits cookies until you press play. Playing the video is subject to Google's own privacy policy.

## Children's privacy

This site is not directed at children under 13, and we don't knowingly collect data from them.
